I started to build my first quacopter one month ago, I was really exited to make that and I want to follow this experience with you.

First, I'm going to introduce me. My name is Pauline, I'm Belgian and 14 years old. For my last birthday, I received a quadcopter. That was a small and cool quacopter and one year later, I decided to build my own. In this website, I'm going to prove you that's possible for a girl, a teenager, an old people and everyone who wants to, to make that. This website will be serparted in three parts : the material, the build, and the fly.
